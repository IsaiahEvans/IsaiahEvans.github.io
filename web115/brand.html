Streamline Studios Brand Guide

Business Name

Streamline Studios

Business Concept

Streamline Studios is a creative media business specializing in automotive photography, portrait photography, and videography content creation. The goal of the brand is to provide high quality cinematic visuals for individuals, car enthusiasts, and businesses.

Mission Statement

My mission is to create visually compelling media that helps people showcase their personality, brand, and passion through professional photography and video production.

Color Scheme

  • Primary: Black (#111111)
  • Accent: Blue (#2d6cdf)
  • Neutral: Light Gray (#f2f2f2)

Font Choices

  • Headings: Arial Bold
  • Body Text: Arial / Helvetica

Tagline

"Capture the Vision."

Logo

The Streamline Studios logo uses the initials "SS" in a clean, modern design. It represents creativity, media production, and automotive photography while remaining simple and readable across different devices and screen sizes. Clicking the logo in the header returns the user to the home page.

Favicon

The favicon is a simplified version of the logo used in the browser tab. It helps users easily recognize the website when multiple tabs are open and provides consistent branding across the site.
